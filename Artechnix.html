More About Us

Our Services

Artechnix is a full service production house. We offer the entire gamut of the production life cycle.

Production Services

Artechnix offers production planning scheduling, crew staffing, budgeting, and production management services.

Equipment

Artechnix is a pioneer in the realm of digital cinema in India. We take great pride in the skills of our trained staff. Our USP is the positive attitude of our personnel.

Post Production Services

Artechnix Studios offers editing, VFX, color grading, DI services and facilities. We have collaborations with multiple vendors to cover any other needs to fulfill your needs as a one stop solution.

Creative Services

Artechnix has a team of creative personnel that provide all aspects of bringing dreams to the silver screen. Writing, Direction, Cinematography, Music Composition, Choreography ... We can fill the gaps in your creative team or help you build one from scratch.

Who Are We
